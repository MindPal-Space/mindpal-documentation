# How to Activate Your Paid Plan on MindPal: A Complete Guide

MindPal offers a straightforward process to activate your paid plan, whether you're using a personal or team workspace. Here's a comprehensive guide to help you get started with your premium features.

## Activating Personal Pro Plan

The process for activating your personal pro plan is quick and simple:

1.  **Access Settings Page**
    
    *   Navigate to [https://brain.mindpal.space/personal/settings](https://brain.mindpal.space/personal/settings)
        
    *   If you're new to MindPal, you'll need to create an account by clicking "Sign up" first
        
2.  **License Key Entry**
    
    *   Locate your unique license key from:
        
        *   The order email receipt sent to your registered email address, OR
            
        *   Your orders page at [https://app.lemonsqueezy.com/my-orders](https://app.lemonsqueezy.com/my-orders)
            
    *   Find the "Activate your plan with a valid license key" input field
        
    *   Paste your license key into this field
        
3.  **Activation Confirmation**
    
    *   Once entered correctly, your personal workspace will be automatically activated with your paid plan features
        

## Activating Team & Business Plan

For team and business plans, the activation process involves a few additional steps:

1.  **Initial Access**
    
    *   Go to [https://brain.mindpal.space](https://brain.mindpal.space)
        
    *   Create an account if you haven't already done so
        
2.  **Team Workspace Setup**
    
    *   Navigate to the team workspace where you want to activate the paid plan
        
    *   If you need to create a new team workspace:
        
        *   Click the workspace switcher button on the left sidebar
            
        *   Select "New team" to create a fresh team workspace
            
3.  **Plan Activation**
    
    *   While in your team workspace, locate and click "Upgrade" on the left sidebar
        
    *   This will take you to the Plan & Billing page
        
4.  **License Key Entry**
    
    *   Obtain your unique license key from either:
        
        *   Your order email receipt, OR
            
        *   [https://app.lemonsqueezy.com/my-orders](https://app.lemonsqueezy.com/my-orders)
            
    *   Find the "Activate your plan with a valid license key" input field
        
    *   Paste your license key
        
5.  **Confirmation**
    
    *   Upon successful entry, your team workspace will be activated with all paid plan features
        

## Important Notes

*   Keep your license key safe as it's unique to your subscription
    
*   Make sure you're logged into the correct workspace when activating a team plan
    
*   If you encounter any issues during activation, contact MindPal's support team for assistance
    

By following these steps, you'll be able to quickly activate your paid plan and start enjoying MindPal's premium features. Whether you're using it for personal productivity or team collaboration, your enhanced MindPal experience awaits.
