## Use cases of AI for Education

Here are what most educators, and education institutions use MindPal for:

### 1. Building custom AI to automate administrative tasks for teachers

Some administrative tasks at school such as writing a lesson plan, writing student reports, writing feedback can be automated once you build custom AI workflow that work specifically for your work purpose or your organization.

1. **AI workflow for Lesson Planning** helps teachers efficiently create customized lesson plans. This template workflow defines topics and goals, gathers relevant resources, and designs engaging activities tailored to specific grade levels and learning objectives.

2. **AI workflow for Student Reports** helps teachers create personalized reports by analyzing student performance, identifying strengths and weaknesses, and determining areas for improvement.

3. **AI workflow for Grading and Giving Personalized Feedback** helps teachers grade assignment tasks, then provide detailed personalized feedback based on a given rubric.

### 2. Building AI tutors for students

Educators and schools often explore building interactive AI tutors in the form of chatbots for their students. MindPal's AI chatbot builder allows you to build a tutor that can teach, give feedback, and generate quizzes, based on your instruction and materials.
