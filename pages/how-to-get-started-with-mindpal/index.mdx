# How to Get Started with MindPal

Welcome to MindPal! This platform is designed to help you create AI agents and multi-agent workflows that can automate a variety of tasks. To get started, you'll first want to familiarize yourself with **agents**, which are the intelligent helpers that perform specific tasks for you. Then, explore **workflows**, which allow you to connect multiple agents together to handle more complex processes. By combining these two powerful features, you can enhance your productivity and streamline your tasks effortlessly. Let's dive in and unlock the full potential of MindPal!
